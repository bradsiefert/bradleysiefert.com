About

Bio

Hello, I'm a Chicago based graphic designer and photographer available for portraits, documentary and editorial work. My favorite kind of photography is extended travel photo stories, especially outdoor photography work. My strengths are in balancing documentary and editorial style photography often combining studio strobes on location.
